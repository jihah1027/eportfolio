Fashion

Featured Creator: The Chic Maven

Kristen Bradley, known as The Chic Maven, is a Miami-based entrepreneur who blends her expertise in finance with a passion for luxury fashion and event design. She is the founder of Panache Style, a floral and event design company, and the creator of the The Chic Maven YouTube channel, where she shares styling tips and luxury fashion advice.

Her YouTube channel, launched in 2021, focuses on wardrobe investments, classic style, and grooming. With a background in pageantry and retail (Neiman Marcus, Forever 21), she offers a practical yet elegant approach to fashion.

Through her company, Panache Style, Kristen has designed events for major brands like Samsung and Capital One, with work featured at NFL Honors and Super Bowl events.

She holds a finance degree from the University of Houston and encourages others to pursue their purpose with confidence and intention.
